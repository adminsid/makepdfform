Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I hit my submission limit?

If you reach your monthly submission limit, we'll notify you via email. Your forms will continue to work for a grace period of 48 hours, giving you time to upgrade to the next tier without interrupting your data collection.

Can I remove the watermark on the Free plan?

The "Made with MakePDFForm" watermark is mandatory on the Free plan. To remove it and present a fully professional look to your clients, you'll need to upgrade to the Pro plan or higher.

How does the real-time collaboration work?

Similar to Google Docs, our Team and Business plans allow multiple users to edit a PDF form simultaneously. You can see your team members' cursors and changes in real-time, preventing version conflicts.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes. If you're not satisfied with MakePDFForm for any reason within the first 14 days of your subscription, simply contact support for a full refund—no questions asked.
